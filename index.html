Green Legacy Initiative

Vision

Ensure the development of the country through implementing efficient rural as well as urban green initiatives by coordinating human power and institutional & Technical capacities at all levels

Mission

Planting seedlings in cities and rural areas to improve the benefit of forest for the country's social and ecological goals and realizing Ethiopia's prosperity.

Planting Targets

Ethiopia planned to plant twenty Billion trees in 4 years

Citizens

More than 16 million citizens are participated in tree planting on tree planting program since 2019.

Land Coverage

More than 50 million forestry and 34,800 million agro forestry seedlings were panted in last four years with coverage of 1.2 million hectare lands
